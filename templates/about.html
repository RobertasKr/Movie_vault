About Us

Welcome to Movie Vault, your one-stop destination for discovering, organizing, and reviewing movies!

Our platform connects you to a vast database of films, using The Movie Database (TMDB) API to bring you detailed and up-to-date information on movies from around the world. Whether you're a casual moviegoer or a dedicated cinephile, we offer everything you need to explore and keep track of the films that matter to you.

What You Can Do:

  • Search Movies: Instantly find any movie you're curious about. With TMDB’s extensive library, you can explore films from the latest blockbusters to timeless classics.
  • Create Your Watchlist: Easily add movies to your personal watchlist, so you never forget what you want to watch next.
  • Leave Reviews: Share your thoughts and opinions on the movies you’ve watched. Your reviews help others decide what to watch next.

This website was built as the final project for my CodeAcademy courses, where I applied the skills I’ve learned in web development and Python to create an interactive and dynamic platform for movie lovers.

I’m excited to share this project with you and look forward to growing the community of film enthusiasts!
